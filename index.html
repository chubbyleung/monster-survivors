About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where your skills and strategy determine how long you can last. Face waves of increasingly challenging monsters while collecting power-ups and upgrading your character's abilities.

Game Features

Endless Adventure

Experience non-stop action with endless waves of unique monsters and challenges.

Character Progression

Unlock powerful abilities and upgrades as you progress through the game.

Strategic Gameplay

Choose your upgrades wisely and adapt your strategy to survive longer.

High Score Challenge

Compete for the highest survival time and challenge yourself to beat your records.

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Automatically attack nearby enemies
  • Collect experience orbs to level up
  • Choose power-ups wisely to create powerful combinations
  • Survive as long as possible!
